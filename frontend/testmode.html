More About Us

How do I join Savanna Fibre?

To join Savanna Fibre network, you can click on the link provided and fill in a request form. After the form is filled, a Savanna telesales representative will reach out and assist you to complete the process. You can also call us for assistance.

Billing and Payment

How do I know my account number?

How do I know my account number? You can use USSD or the Savanna App/Adopt App.

How do I pay for my monthly subscription?

Payment can be made via Mpesa, Tigo Pesa, Airtel Money, or through our website. How do I upgrade or downgrade my package? A request can be made through the USSD code, Savanna App, or Adopt App.

How do I change my WiFi username and password?

Login to the modem via cable using a laptop. Default IP is normally 192.168.0.1. If changed, use 'ipconfig' in a terminal to find the default gateway IP. Change credentials via the Adopt App or browser.

Internet Service Issues

If experiencing issues, ensure cables are tightly plugged, IP addresses are set to obtain automatically, and Wi-Fi keys are entered correctly. Restart the modem if necessary.

Installation and Setup

Installation is completed the same day after approval. Provided equipment includes a router and necessary cables, with no installation fee.
